Volleyball

A net separates the two teams of six players in the well-liked team sport of volleyball. By placing the ball on the court of the opposing team, one can score points. The serve begins the match, which lasts until the ball is out of bounds or a team makes a poor return. In addition to physical ability, such as jumping, hitting, and blocking, volleyball also calls for strategic thinking and teamwork. It is a well-liked sport in many nations throughout the world and is played at different levels, including recreational, high school, college, and professional.
