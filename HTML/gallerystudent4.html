Volleyball is a well-liked team sport in which two teams of six players each compete against one another over a net. The aim of the game is to ground the ball on the other team's court by sending it over the net while thwarting their attempts to do the same. To play the game, players combine spiking, blocking, serving, digging, and setting techniques. To play volleyball, you need the following equipment:
• Volleyball
• Net
• Volleyball Shoes
• Protective Gear
• Accessories
